Understanding RA 10173 and RA 10175

This paper provides an overview of Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) and Republic Act No. 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012), explaining their purpose, key provisions, and real-world applications. It discusses the different types of personal data, the rights of data subjects, the responsibilities of organizations and Data Protection Officers, and the penalties for violations under RA 10173, with oversight by the National Privacy Commission.
